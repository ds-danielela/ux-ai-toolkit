# Design principles

> Source: https://zeroheight.com/0b922d40b/v/latest/p/9347af-design-principles

### 01 Guide the user throughout the way

Provide frequent feedback, and guide the user to achieve their goals along the user journey.

### 02 Ground (all) design decisions on a deep understanding of user needs & problems

Design with empathy, relying on usage data, user research and testing. When in doubt, return to the user for guidance.

### 03 Context matters

Always be mindful of the users context and emotional state. Consider the way users naturally understand and interact with interfaces through their own lens (i.e Mental models).

### 04 Complex doesn't always mean complicated

Design clear visual layouts and hierarchy to ensure ease of use, enabling users to focus on their objectives. Support the beginners, while empowering the experts by surfacing the right amount of complexity they need at the right time.

### 05 Keep the user at the heart of everything you do

Design professional trustworthy products that always keep the benefit of the user at heart and enables them to be most productive. Avoid advertisement, dark patterns, spam or deliberately misleading the user.

### 06 Engage and tell a story

Provide feedback through engaging visuals and storytelling to build user trust and manage expectations.

### 07 Look around and ahead

Strive to solve today's problems using tomorrows approaches. Keep your eyes open to learn from different approaches and industries. Don't be afraid to ask "Why not" to push and redefine the boundaries.

### 08 Craft everything you do with love

... or don't do it at all. That's the only way to truly delight the user.
