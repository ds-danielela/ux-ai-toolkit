# Spot illustrations

> Source: https://zeroheight.com/0b922d40b/v/latest/p/79c3c6-spot-illustrations
> Introduction: Spot Illustrations are simple, visuals that represent objects, concepts, or actions in a clear and engaging way. They are primarily used in empty states to provide context, enhance visual appeal, and guide users toward their next steps.

## Sizes

The spot illustrations can be used in two different sizes:

* **S**: 96px
* **M**: 120px

---

## Style

* **Form & structure**: Spot illustrations maintain a minimal, clean, and modern aesthetic with soft edges and a balanced composition.
* **Colors**: The primary colors are white, light blue, and chartreuse, ensuring a fresh and approachable feel. The use of color is purposeful, with blue as the main symbol color and chartreuse as an accent.
* **Lighting & shadows**: Soft, even lighting gives the illustrations depth while maintaining a subtle 3D effect. Shadows are minimal to keep a lightweight visual presence.
* **Metallic accents**: Spot illustrations feature small metallic elements (e.g., clips, rings, or bindings). These details add a realistic touch and should remain subtle and consistent across all illustrations.
* **Consistency**: The perspective, proportions, and stroke weight of elements should remain uniform to maintain coherence across all illustrations.

## Usage

For the designers, all spot illustrations are provided as components in the Graphics & Illustrations Figma library. To access the icons, the library must be included.

The components are created in a way that they can scale so that they can also be used in the small size.

### Export settings

To ensure spot illustrations maintain clarity and visual quality across different screen sizes and resolutions, they should be exported in 1.0x, 2.0x and 3.0x size.

The assets for 2.0x and 3.0x should be provided in subfolders (see [Resolution-aware image assets](https://docs.flutter.dev/ui/assets/assets-and-images#resolution-aware)).

### Do's and don'ts

- **Do**: Only use the spot illustrations in the supported size (S: 96px, M: 120px).
- **Don't**: Scale to other sizes outside of S and M.
- **Do**: Use illustrations as-is with their defined color palette.
- **Don't**: Modify colors or add effects that break the unified style.
